🔥 Daily Fat-Burning Workout Plan

Total Time: ~30–35 minutes

Format: Warm-up → Main Workout (3 circuits) → Core Finisher → Cooldown

🔥 1. Warm-Up (5 mins)

Do each for 30 seconds × 2 rounds

  • Jumping Jacks
  • High Knees
  • Arm Circles (forward + backward)
  • Leg Swings
  • Shoulder Rolls

💪 2. Main Fat-Burning Workout (3 Circuits)

🔁 Circuit 1: Lower Body Burn (2–3 rounds)

  • 20 Bodyweight Squats
  • 15 Jump Squats
  • 12 Lunges each leg
  • 30 sec Wall Sit
Rest 30–45 sec after each round

🔁 Circuit 2: Upper Body + Cardio (2–3 rounds)

  • 12 Incline Push-ups (or full push-ups if able)
  • 10 Burpees
  • 20 Mountain Climbers
  • 10 Shoulder Taps each side
Rest 30–45 sec after each round

🔁 Circuit 3: HIIT Core + Cardio (2–3 rounds)

  • 20 Russian Twists (10 each side)
  • 15 Leg Raises
  • 30 sec Plank
  • 10 Flutter Kicks each leg
Rest 30 sec after each round

🧨 3. Core Finisher (Optional – Hardcore Belly Focus)

Do this once or twice

  • 30 sec Hollow Body Hold
  • 20 Bicycle Crunches
  • 20 sec Side Plank (each side)
  • 10 Slow Controlled Sit-ups

🧘 4. Cooldown & Stretch (5 mins)

  • 30 sec Child's Pose
  • 30 sec Cobra Stretch (for abs)
  • 30 sec Seated Forward Fold (hamstrings)
  • 30 sec Shoulder stretch
  • 1 min Deep Breathing (lying flat)

💡 Tips for Best Results:

  • Push hard during working sets, keep rests short.
  • Repeat 5–6 days per week for maximum fat burn.
  • Track calories and eat in a calorie deficit.
  • Add jump rope for 5–10 mins = extra 80–120 kcal burn.
